The Problem

Every business loses customers to unanswered messages.

When someone messages at 9pm asking if you have a table Saturday, they want an answer now — not tomorrow morning. Most of the time they move on and book somewhere else.

The same happens with questions about prices, opening hours, allergens, and walk-in availability. Staff are busy. Phones go unanswered. Messages pile up.

An AI agent replies instantly, any time of day or night, using the information you give us about your business. It sounds natural, knows your menu and your prices, and only passes things to you when it genuinely needs a human.

Who It's For

Works for the businesses that get the most messages

Every business type has the same problem — too many routine messages and not enough time to answer them all.

Restaurants and takeaways

Customers message to check availability, ask about the menu, and make special requests. Most of those messages arrive in the evenings and at weekends — exactly when you are busiest.

Example message your agent handles
"Hi do you have a table for 4 on Saturday evening? It's my mum's birthday so would love somewhere nice."

Your agent replies instantly, checks availability, takes the booking, notes the occasion, and sends a confirmation — all without you touching your phone.

Cafés and coffee shops

Pre-orders, opening hours, allergen questions, and daily specials come in before and during your busiest hours — when you have the least time to reply.

Example message your agent handles
"What time do you open on Sunday? And do you do oat milk?"

Your agent answers immediately and can send the day's special to anyone who opts in — before they've even left the house.

Barbers and salons

Availability questions and booking requests come in constantly — including while you are in the middle of a cut and cannot pick up your phone.

Example message your agent handles
"Any slots free this Saturday morning? Ideally before 11."

Your agent checks your availability, offers suitable times, and confirms the booking. If someone cancels, it can message the next person on the waiting list automatically.

Gyms and fitness studios

Class availability, membership questions, and drop-in rates get asked dozens of times a week. Your agent handles every one of them — and reminds members who have gone quiet to come back.

Example message your agent handles
"Is there space in the Tuesday 6:30 class? And what's the drop-in price?"

Your agent checks availability, answers the pricing question, and can send a follow-up a few weeks later if the member has not booked again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

Does my customer know they are talking to an AI?

Yes. The agent introduces itself as an automated assistant for your business. It sounds natural and helpful — but it is honest about what it is. This is also a requirement of WhatsApp's business platform.

What if a customer asks something the agent doesn't know?

The agent is trained to recognise when a question is outside what it can handle. In that case it tells the customer you will be in touch shortly and sends you a notification so nothing falls through the cracks.

Does it replace my WhatsApp number?

No. The agent connects to your existing WhatsApp Business number. You can still see all conversations and step in any time you want.

How quickly can the agent be updated?

The same way you update your screens — send us a WhatsApp message. Price changes, new menu items, holiday hours — we update the agent within one business day.

Does it work outside of WhatsApp?

Yes. The Starter and Pro plans both include a website chat widget so the same agent works on your website too. The Starter plan also includes Facebook Messenger, so your Facebook page is covered as well.

What if I already have a ScreenFizz screen plan?

You can add the AI agent to your existing plan from £35 per month. Get in touch and we will sort it out on the same invoice.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes. Get in touch and we can walk you through a live demo using a real example for your type of business — before you pay anything.
