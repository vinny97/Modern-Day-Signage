What It Means

What are digital advertising signs?

Digital advertising signs are screens in or around your business that show promotional content, menus, offers, prices or customer information. They replace printed posters, static menus and handwritten boards with professional, updateable digital content.

In a shop window, they attract passing trade and drive footfall. Behind a counter, they guide purchase decisions and promote your best-margin items. In a waiting area, they keep customers occupied and informed.

ScreenFizz manages your advertising screens as a fully done-for-you service. We supply the hardware, design the content, and update it whenever you need a change. You focus on running your business.

FAQ

Common questions

What content can I show on a digital advertising screen?

Anything you want to communicate to customers. Menus, prices, offers, promotions, opening hours, social media handles, product highlights, or seasonal campaigns. We design it all for you.

How do I update what is on the screen?

Send us a WhatsApp message with what you want to change. We will update your screen and confirm when it is live, usually the same day.

Do I need to buy a TV or screen?

You provide the TV. We supply and configure the media player that plugs into it and controls what is displayed. We can also arrange professional screen installation if needed.

How much do digital advertising screens cost to run?

ScreenFizz starts from £15 per month per screen, which covers all content updates, monitoring, and support. The one-time hardware cost is from £60.
