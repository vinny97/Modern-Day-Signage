Common questions

Digital menu board FAQ

Can I use my existing TV?

Often, yes. Send us the model and a photo of your space and we’ll check its suitability and recommend any player or mounting hardware needed.

Can you supply displays?

Yes. We can source commercial displays matched to your space, opening hours and budget.

Can you install?

Yes. Professional installation is available, or we can prepare a simple plug-in setup for your own installer.

Can I change prices every day?

Yes. Send changes by WhatsApp or email and we’ll update the relevant menu content remotely.

How quickly can updates be made?

Most straightforward text and price changes can be handled the same working day. We’ll confirm timing when you send the request.

We’ll recommend the right screens and setup for your venue.
