What It Means

What does digital signage do for a restaurant?

Digital signage in a restaurant covers everything from the menu board behind the counter to promotional screens near the entrance, to specials boards visible from the tables. Each screen is an opportunity to communicate with customers and influence what they order.

Research consistently shows that well-designed menu boards increase average order values. When customers can see your best dishes prominently displayed with attractive descriptions, they are more likely to try something new or add an extra item.

ScreenFizz manages restaurant digital signage as a complete done-for-you service. We supply the hardware, design your content, and handle all updates. When your lunch special changes or a dish sells out, send us a WhatsApp and your screen reflects it the same day.

FAQ

Common questions

What type of screens do restaurants typically use?

Most restaurants use screens mounted behind the counter for menu boards, plus promotional screens near the entrance or on tables. The right setup depends on your layout, which we help you plan.

Can I have different menus at different times of day?

Yes. We set up time-based scheduling so your screens automatically show the right menu for the time of day, whether that is breakfast, lunch, dinner, or a late-night menu.

How quickly can my screen be updated when the menu changes?

Same day in most cases. Send us a WhatsApp message with the changes and we will update your screen and confirm it is live.
