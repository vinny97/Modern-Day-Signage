Can we show a welcome message for specific visitors or clients?

Yes. WhatsApp us the visitor or client name and we update the welcome screen for their arrival. It is a simple touch that makes a strong impression when guests arrive at reception.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can we have different screens in different areas of the office showing relevant content?

Yes. Reception, canteen, meeting floors, each screen can show different content managed independently. One WhatsApp message can update all of them or just one.
