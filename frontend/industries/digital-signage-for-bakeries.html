Can the screen show what is available today and update when items sell out?

Yes. You can WhatsApp us when something sells out or when a new batch comes out of the oven, and we update the screen the same day. Many bakery customers update their screens two or three times a week to keep content accurate.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can we promote our custom celebration cake service on the screen?

Yes. A dedicated panel for custom cakes, with photos of your work and a prompt to enquire, is one of the best ways to generate orders from customers who are already in your shop.
