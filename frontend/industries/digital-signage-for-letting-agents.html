Can we update the window display immediately when a property is let?

Yes, same day. WhatsApp us and we update the display, replacing the available listing with a 'just let' banner or removing it entirely. Your window always reflects your live availability.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can the screen promote our landlord services as well as rental listings?

Yes. We can alternate between available properties and a promotion for your full property management service, so the screen attracts both tenants and landlords from foot traffic.
