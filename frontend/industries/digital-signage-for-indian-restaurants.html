Can we clearly mark vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free dishes on the board?

Yes. We include V, VG and GF labels as part of the menu design so customers with dietary requirements can order confidently without needing to ask staff for every dish.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can we promote our lunch thali and evening à la carte separately?

Yes. Your screen can switch automatically between your lunch and dinner menus at a set time, so customers always see the right menu for when they are dining.
