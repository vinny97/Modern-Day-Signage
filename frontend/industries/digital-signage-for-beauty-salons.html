Can the screen help promote treatments clients might not know about?

Absolutely. Many clients book the same treatment every visit without knowing what else you offer. A screen showing your full treatment menu, including facials, body treatments, or advanced skin services, naturally encourages clients to try something new.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can the screen promote gift vouchers around Christmas and Valentine's Day?

Yes. We update your content seasonally, festive gift voucher promotions in November and December, Valentine's packages in February, Mother's Day offers in March. We plan these in advance so you never miss a commercial moment.
