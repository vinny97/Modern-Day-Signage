Can the screen run seasonal campaigns automatically, like flea treatment reminders in spring?

Yes. We plan your seasonal content calendar in advance, flea season in spring, fireworks advice in October, festive pet safety in December. Your screen stays relevant without you needing to prompt us.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Is a waiting room screen appropriate for a clinical veterinary environment?

Yes. We design content that is calm and informative, pet care tips, health plan information, seasonal advice, rather than loud or promotional. It keeps waiting clients informed without feeling out of place in a healthcare setting.
