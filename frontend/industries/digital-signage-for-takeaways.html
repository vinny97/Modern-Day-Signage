Can we update the menu board when our prices change?

Absolutely. Just send us a WhatsApp message with the new price or item and it will be updated on your screen the same day. No need to reprint menus or update any software yourself.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can we show allergen information on the menu board?

Yes. We include allergen labels (gluten-free, vegan, nuts, dairy etc.) as part of the menu layout. This helps customers with dietary requirements order with confidence and reduces questions for your staff.
