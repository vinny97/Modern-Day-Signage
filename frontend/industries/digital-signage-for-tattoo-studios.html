Can each artist's work be displayed separately on the screen?

Yes. We can create a rotation that showcases each artist's portfolio individually, so clients waiting can see who specialises in what and start thinking about who they want to book with.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can we update the screen to announce a guest artist or flash day?

Yes, same day. WhatsApp us the details and artwork and we update your screen the same day. A guest artist announcement on your screen gives it extra visibility with clients already in the studio.
