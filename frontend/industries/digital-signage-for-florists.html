Updates via WhatsApp

Send us a message anytime. Price change, new promotion, seasonal update, done the same day.

Can we update the screen to promote Valentine's Day or Mother's Day bouquets in advance?

Yes. We plan seasonal promotions in advance, Valentine's in January, Mother's Day in February, Christmas wreaths in November, so your screen promotes the right thing at the right time without you needing to remind us.

Do I need technical knowledge to use ScreenFizz?

None at all. You never log in to any software or touch any settings. If you want something changed on your screen, you WhatsApp us and we handle it.

Can we show photos of our actual work rather than stock images?

Yes, and we recommend it. Photos of your real arrangements are far more effective than stock images. Send us photos via WhatsApp and we add them to your screen rotation.
