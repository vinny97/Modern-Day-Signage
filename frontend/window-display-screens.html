What It Means

What are window display screens?

Window display screens are digital displays placed in a shop window facing outward, visible to people walking past. They replace static window posters, printed offers, and hand-written signs with professional, high-brightness digital content that changes whenever you need it to.

A well-managed window screen acts as a 24-hour salesperson. It communicates your offers, promotes your services, and drives people through the door who might otherwise have walked past. The content updates the moment your promotions change.

ScreenFizz supplies the player, designs your window content, and keeps it updated throughout the year. New seasonal campaign, new product launch, or a simple price change, you send us a WhatsApp and the window screen reflects it the same day.

FAQ

Common questions

Do window display screens work in direct sunlight?

Standard screens can wash out in bright sunlight. We advise on high-brightness screen options for south-facing windows and help you choose the right hardware for your situation.

Can I show different content in the window than inside the shop?

Yes. Window-facing and in-store screens are managed separately. Each shows exactly what makes sense for its location.

How do I update my window display?

Send a WhatsApp message with the new content or change you need. We update the screen and confirm it is live, usually the same day.

How much does a window display screen cost?

ScreenFizz starts from £15 per month per screen with a one-time hardware cost from £60. Window campaigns, seasonal content, and all updates are included.
