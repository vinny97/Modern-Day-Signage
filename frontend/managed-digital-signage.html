What It Means

What is managed digital signage?

Managed digital signage means a company handles your screens on your behalf. Instead of buying software, learning how to use it, and updating it yourself, you pay a monthly fee and a team manages everything for you.

With ScreenFizz, that means we supply the media player, configure it before it arrives, arrange installation if needed, design your initial content, and keep it updated whenever you need a change. You never log in to any software or touch any settings.

When you want something new on your screens, such as a price change, a new promotion, or a seasonal update, you send us a message on WhatsApp. We make the update and confirm it is live, usually the same day.
