What It Means

What does digital signage content creation involve?

Digital signage content creation is the design and production of the graphics, text, and layouts that appear on your business screens. This includes your initial menu board design, promotional graphics, seasonal campaigns, and any updates you need throughout the year.

Most business owners are not designers. Using generic templates or publishing raw photos does not produce the professional result that actually converts customers. Content that is branded consistently, legible at a distance, and designed to highlight the right information makes a measurable difference.

With ScreenFizz, content creation is part of the service. We design your initial screen layouts, refresh them when your offering changes, and produce seasonal campaigns at key times of year. You send us what you want to communicate and we handle how it looks.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to provide design files or images?

No. We work from whatever you can send us, a photo of your price list, a text message with your menu, or a brief description of what you want to promote. We handle the design from there.

How do I request new content or changes?

Send us a WhatsApp message. Tell us what you want to change or add and we will design and publish the update, usually the same day.

Can you match my existing branding?

Yes. We can work from your logo, brand colours, and any style guidelines you have. If you do not have formal guidelines, we will create something clean and consistent that suits your business.

Is content creation included in the monthly fee?

Yes. All content updates and seasonal refreshes are included in the ScreenFizz monthly plan. There are no per-update charges.
