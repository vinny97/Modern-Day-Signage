ScreenFizz vs OptiSigns

OptiSigns is a well-known digital signage platform popular with cafes, restaurants, and retailers. It is solid software, but it is still software you manage yourself. ScreenFizz is a fully managed service. You do not log in, you do not design content, you do not touch any settings. You just run your business.

The key difference

OptiSigns gives you a platform to display content on your screens. It has templates, scheduling tools, and a content editor. But using it still requires you to learn the software, create or upload your own content, and update it yourself whenever anything changes.

ScreenFizz works differently. You tell us what you need, a menu board, a window display, a promotional screen, and we set it up for you. When your prices change or you want to add a new offer, you send us a WhatsApp message. We make the update and confirm it is live. You never log into anything.

For most independent business owners, that difference matters. Time spent managing signage software is time not spent on customers.

When OptiSigns makes sense

OptiSigns is a good fit if you enjoy managing your own screens, have a team member who can update content regularly, and want a large library of templates to work from. It supports a wide range of hardware and has a straightforward interface for non-technical users.

When ScreenFizz is the better fit

  • You want it done for you. ScreenFizz is a managed service, you never log in to any software. We handle setup, content, and ongoing maintenance.
  • You need TV installation. We arrange professional installation across the UK, including evenings and weekends to avoid disruption.
  • Updates via WhatsApp. No design software, no template editor, no logins. Send us a message and your screen is updated the same day.
  • You want a UK-based team. ScreenFizz is UK-based and focused entirely on UK businesses.

Pricing comparison

OptiSigns starts at around £10 per screen per month for the software. On top of that, you need hardware (typically £80–200+ for a player) and you are responsible for all content creation and updates yourself.

ScreenFizz starts at £15/month per screen. That price includes software, content management, screen monitoring, and same-day updates via WhatsApp. Hardware is £60 one-time. No hidden costs, no time investment required.
