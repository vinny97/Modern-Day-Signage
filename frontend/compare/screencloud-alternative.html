ScreenFizz vs ScreenCloud

ScreenCloud is an enterprise digital signage platform built for large organisations with in-house technical teams. ScreenFizz is a fully managed service built for independent businesses and we handle everything so you do not have to.

The key difference

ScreenCloud is software. It gives you a platform to build content, manage screens, and publish playlists, but all of that work falls on you. It is designed for companies with dedicated marketing or IT staff who can run a digital signage system day-to-day.

ScreenFizz takes a different approach. We are a managed service, which means we handle setup, content, updates, and monitoring. You tell us what you want on your screen, via WhatsApp, email, or a quick call, and we make it happen. No software to learn, no logins to manage.

ScreenCloud is also built for large enterprise customers, with pricing to match. ScreenFizz is built for independent UK businesses, cafes, restaurants, barbers, retailers, who want professional screens without enterprise complexity or cost.

When ScreenCloud makes sense

ScreenCloud is a reasonable choice for larger organisations, office buildings, corporate campuses, or retail chains, that have a dedicated team to manage their digital signage. It has strong integrations with enterprise tools like Slack, Power BI, and Google Drive. If you have an IT team and dozens of screens across multiple sites, ScreenCloud is built for that context.

When ScreenFizz is the better fit

  • You are a small or independent business. ScreenCloud is priced and designed for enterprise. ScreenFizz is built for cafes, restaurants, salons, and retailers who want professional screens without the complexity.
  • You do not want to manage software. ScreenFizz is a done-for-you service. No content management system to learn, no design software, no technical setup.
  • You need installation. We arrange TV installation across the UK as part of the service. ScreenCloud does not offer this.
  • You want to update content via WhatsApp. Our customers send us a message when something changes. That is it.

Pricing comparison

ScreenCloud's pricing is not publicly listed but typically starts at £40 or more per screen per month, targeted at enterprise contracts. For a small business with one or two screens, this is significant.

ScreenFizz starts at £15/month per screen. That covers software, content management, screen monitoring, and same-day updates. The hardware player is £60 one-time.
