What It Means

What is a digital menu board?

A digital menu board is a screen behind or above your counter showing your menu, prices, and any promotions. It replaces printed or chalkboard menus with a professional, easily updateable display.

Done well, digital menu boards reduce perceived wait times, highlight your best-margin items, and let you switch between breakfast and lunch menus automatically without touching anything. Done badly, they just show a static image that nobody updates.

ScreenFizz manages your digital menu boards as a complete service. We design the initial layout, set up the hardware, and update your menu whenever your prices or items change. You send us a WhatsApp and it is live the same day.

Send us a WhatsApp message with the changes you need. We will update your board and confirm it is live, usually within a few hours.

Can the menu board show different content at different times?

Yes. We can set up time-based scheduling so your breakfast menu shows in the morning and switches to your lunch or dinner menu automatically.

Do I need design skills to use a digital menu board?

No. We design your menu board for you as part of the service. Just send us your menu and prices and we will create a professional layout.
