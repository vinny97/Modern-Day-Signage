Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

1. Who We Are

ScreenFizz ("we", "us", "our") is a UK-based managed digital signage service. We help small businesses display professional content on their screens without any technical effort on their part. For privacy enquiries, contact us at info@screenfizz.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide and improve our service:

2.1 Enquiry and Contact Data

2.2 Customer Account Data

If you become a ScreenFizz customer, we also hold your business name, billing address, and subscription details to deliver and invoice the service.

2.3 Website Usage

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics tools that profile you individually. We may log basic server access data (page requests, error codes) for security and performance monitoring. This data is not tied to your identity.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Legitimate interests — responding to your enquiry and providing the service you requested
  • Contract performance — processing your order and managing your subscription
  • Legal obligation — retaining billing records as required by UK law
  • Consent — where we send marketing communications, you can opt out at any time

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your setup enquiry and provide a personalised quote
  • Set up and manage your digital signage displays
  • Send content updates, invoices, and service notifications
  • Improve our service based on aggregate feedback (no individual profiling)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only where necessary:

  • Payment processing — Stripe, for billing (they are PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Email delivery — Resend, to send transactional emails on our behalf
  • Hosting — our servers are hosted on Render and Supabase within secure, GDPR-compliant data centres
  • Legal requirements — if required by UK law, court order, or regulatory authority

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond delivering the service.

6. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed — never stored in plain text
  • Access to customer data is restricted to authorised team members only
  • We use rate limiting and honeypot techniques to prevent spam and abuse on our contact forms

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email info@screenfizz.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

Our marketing website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage only where strictly necessary (for example, to remember your quiz progress). No third-party advertising networks have access to your browsing behaviour on our site.

9. Data Retention

  • Enquiry data: retained for up to 2 years from last contact, then deleted
  • Customer account data: retained for the duration of your subscription plus 6 years (UK accounting requirements)
  • Server logs: 90 days, then automatically deleted

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. For significant changes, we will notify active customers by email.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email us at info@screenfizz.com.
