What It Means

What does fully managed digital signage include?

Fully managed digital signage is a complete, done-for-you service. Unlike software subscriptions where you still have to set things up yourself, a fully managed service handles every part of the process.

At ScreenFizz, fully managed means we supply and configure the media player, help arrange screen installation, create your initial content, and update your screens whenever your needs change. You communicate with us by WhatsApp or email and we handle the rest.

This is the right option for business owners who want professional screens without spending time on technology. There is no software to learn, no design tools to use, and no logins to manage.

FAQ

Common questions

What does fully managed digital signage mean?

It means every part of your digital signage is handled by a service provider. This includes hardware, installation, content design, updates, monitoring, and support. You are not expected to manage any part of it yourself.

How is fully managed different from digital signage software?

Digital signage software gives you a platform to manage your own screens. Fully managed means someone else manages them for you. You do not log in, design content, or handle any technical tasks.

How much does it cost?

ScreenFizz fully managed digital signage starts from £15 per month per screen, with a one-time hardware cost from £60. No contracts and no setup fees.

How do I request content updates?

Just send us a WhatsApp message. Tell us what you want changed, send any images or text, and we will update your screen and confirm when it is live.
