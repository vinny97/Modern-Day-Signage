What It Means

What does a professional digital signage installation include?

Digital signage installation covers everything needed to get a screen up and running in your business. This includes supplying and configuring the media player, advising on screen placement, physically mounting the TV, routing cables neatly, and setting up the initial content before the engineer leaves.

A proper installation matters more than most business owners realise. An improperly mounted screen, loose cables, or an incorrectly configured player can all cause problems that are frustrating and expensive to fix later.

With ScreenFizz, installation is part of the service. Our UK installation partners handle the physical setup professionally, and we configure the player and content remotely so everything is working before the engineer walks out the door.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you install screens across the whole UK?

Yes. We work with installation partners across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Get in touch and we can confirm availability in your area.

Do I need to buy my own TV?

We can advise on the best screen size and type for your space. In some cases we can supply the TV alongside the player, or work with one you already own.

How long does installation take?

A single screen installation typically takes one to two hours. We will confirm an expected time when we book in your installation.

What happens after installation?

Your screen is managed on an ongoing basis as part of the ScreenFizz monthly service. We handle all content updates, monitoring, and support so you never need to touch the system again.
