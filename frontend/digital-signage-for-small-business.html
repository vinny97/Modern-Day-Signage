What It Means

What does digital signage do for a small business?

Digital signage is any screen in your business showing content you control. Menu boards, promotional displays, window screens, waiting room content. Done well, it helps customers understand what you offer, notice your promotions, and spend more.

For small businesses, the challenge has always been the management side. Large chains have marketing teams. Independent businesses do not. That is where a managed service makes a real difference.

With ScreenFizz, your screens are set up and managed for you. When your prices change or you want to run a promotion, you send us a WhatsApp message and we update the screen. No software, no design tools, no logins.
