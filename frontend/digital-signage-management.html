What It Means

What is digital signage management?

Digital signage management is the ongoing work required to keep your business screens useful and up to date. This includes publishing new content, scheduling time-based playlists, monitoring screen health, and responding to any technical issues that come up.

Without management, screens quickly become outdated. A menu with old prices, a promotional screen still showing Christmas offers in February, or a screen that has gone black and nobody noticed. These all damage customer trust and waste the investment.

ScreenFizz takes ongoing management completely off your plate. We monitor your screens remotely, update content whenever you need a change, and run seasonal campaigns at key times of year. You communicate with us by WhatsApp. We handle the rest.

FAQ

Common questions

What does digital signage management include?

It includes monitoring your screens remotely, updating content when you request changes, scheduling time-based playlists, resolving technical issues, and refreshing content for seasonal periods. All of this is included in the monthly fee.

How do I request a content update?

Send a WhatsApp message to our team with the change you need. We will update your screen and confirm when it is live, usually the same day.

What happens if a screen goes offline?

We monitor screen health remotely and will contact you if a screen goes offline or shows a fault. In most cases we can diagnose and resolve the issue without a site visit.
